Balanced Diet

  A healthy diet is good for your physical and mental health. It can reduce the risk and severity of obesity, heart disease, diabetes, hypertension, depression and cancer.

Components of a Balanced Diet

A balanced diet should include:

  • Fruits and vegetables
  • Whole grains
  • Protein-rich foods like lean meat, fish, eggs, and beans
  • Dairy products or dairy alternatives
  • Healthy fats like those found in nuts, seeds, and olive oil
  • Plenty of water

Benefits of a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet can:

  • Help maintain a healthy weight
  • Reduce the risk of chronic diseases like heart disease, diabetes, and cancer
  • Improve digestion and overall gut health
  • Boost immunity
  • Provide energy for daily activities

One-Day Balanced Diet Routine

Breakfast

Whole grain cereal with low-fat milk, a serving of fruit (e.g., apple or banana), and a glass of water or green tea.

Morning Snack

Greek yogurt with honey and mixed berries.

Lunch

Grilled chicken breast with quinoa and steamed vegetables, and a side salad with vinaigrette dressing.

Afternoon Snack

Whole grain crackers with hummus.

Dinner

Baked salmon with brown rice and stir-fried broccoli, bell peppers, and carrots.

Evening Snack

A small handful of mixed nuts.

Remember to drink plenty of water throughout the day and adjust portion sizes based on your individual needs.
